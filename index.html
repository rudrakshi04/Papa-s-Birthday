The Story That Defines Everything

He Never Said No

When my USA visa got denied, we had already spent 3-5 lakhs. We stayed at his friend's house in Mumbai for 7 days. I was heartbroken

He looked at me and said, "It's okay. No problem." Even when we both knew it was.

We had planned to celebrate at Trident Hotel, BKC if I got the visa. But when it was rejected, I was crying and just wanted to go home.

Still, he took me there. Ordered the best coffee and dessert.. Clicked my pictures. Did everything he could to make me smile.

That’s my father. He turns pain into memories.

To LOML

Dear Papa,

Today you turn 50. Half a century of fighting, building, sacrificing, and loving.

I don’t think I’ve ever really said this the way I feel it.

You’ve spent your whole life being strong for everyone else… but I’ve seen the quiet parts too. The sacrifices you never talked about. The worries you hid behind a simple “it’s okay.”

You made “it’s okay” feel real, even on the days it wasn’t.

I grew up watching you choose us—again and again—over comfort, over ease, over yourself. And the crazy part? You never made it look like a sacrifice. You made it look like love.

The way you stand behind me makes me feel like I can take on anything in this world. Even when things fall apart, somewhere in my heart I know—*Papa is there*. And that thought alone fixes more than anything else ever could.

I know I don’t say it enough. Maybe I act strong, maybe I act like I’ve got everything handled… but the truth is, a big part of my courage comes from you.

From watching you never give up.

From watching you keep going, no matter what life threw at you.

From knowing that no matter how hard things get—you’ll still choose love.

I don’t need perfect days or big celebrations to remember who you are.

For me, it’s in the smallest things—

the way you care,

the way you show up,

the way you never say no.

You are my safest place.

My biggest strength.

My constant.

And one day, I promise, I’ll make you just as proud as you’ve made me feel every single day of my life.

I love you more than I can ever put into words, Papa..

You never said no to me. Not once. Not ever.

I love you, Papa. You're the best father in the world. And I'm the luckiest daughter

Happy 50th. 🤍

— Rudrakshi
